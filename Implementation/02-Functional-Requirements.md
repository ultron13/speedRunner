# Functional Requirements

## Project And Test Management

- The platform shall support multiple projects, applications, teams, and environments.
- The platform shall provide a centralized catalog for test plans, scripts, scenarios, and load profiles.
- The platform shall support versioned test assets.
- The platform shall support reusable runtime data definitions.

## LoadRunner Enterprise Feature Parity

- The platform shall support centralized performance center-style test management.
- The platform shall support test scheduling, reservation windows, and controlled execution periods.
- The platform shall support distributed load generation.
- The platform shall support load generator groups, pools, labels, and capacity rules.
- The platform shall support real-time test status and live run metrics.
- The platform shall support SLA thresholds for response time, throughput, error rate, and availability.
- The platform shall support result storage, trend comparison, and baseline comparison.
- The platform shall support role-based access by project, team, and environment.
- The platform shall support audit logs for test creation, approval, execution, cancellation, and deletion.

## Better MarathonRunner Features

- The platform shall support Kubernetes-native elastic test execution.
- The platform shall support multiple test engines through an engine abstraction.
- The platform shall support Harbor-managed execution images.
- The platform shall support Redis-based runtime test data pools.
- The platform shall support CI/CD pipeline triggers and release quality gates.
- The platform shall support API-driven execution for automation and integration.
- The platform shall support AI-assisted anomaly detection and bottleneck suggestions.
- The platform shall support multi-region and multi-cluster test execution.
- The platform shall support GitOps-style configuration promotion.

## AI Functional Requirements

- The platform shall generate AI-assisted test design recommendations from business goals.
- The platform shall review test scripts for missing assertions, hard-coded data, weak correlation, missing think time, and poor parameterization.
- The platform shall recommend runtime test data structures and Redis data pool partitioning.
- The platform shall detect anomalies during active and completed test runs.
- The platform shall correlate bottlenecks across load generator, Kubernetes, Redis, application, database, cache, and network telemetry.
- The platform shall generate post-run summaries for engineering, release, SRE, and executive audiences.
- The platform shall support AI-assisted release risk scoring.
- The platform shall support capacity and cost recommendations based on historical results.
- The platform shall enforce RBAC, masking, audit, and approval controls for AI-generated recommendations and actions.
